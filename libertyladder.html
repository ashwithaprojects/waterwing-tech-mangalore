Liberty Ladder

Liberti ladders are light, sturdy and safe. Our expertise in extrusion and control over the complete production processes from raw material to the final ladder and onwards to your kitchen and home help us ensure you climb smart and safely. Liberti ladders are easy to move and need no setting up time. You have multiple choices depending on the chore you would want to do at home. From fixing a bulb to trimming the hedges or plucking that flower, we have ladders of different heights and cater to diverse needs. Simple step stools, stepladders and twin ladders, ladders with a utility tray, multipurpose ladders and articulating ladders. All ladders have a clear rating on the load bearing capacities.
